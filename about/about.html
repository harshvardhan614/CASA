About Us

WHO WE ARE

CASA Kitchens: Where You Build the Kitchen of your Dreams

Our journey started in 2012, under the able hands of Mr. Neeraj Bachhawat, with a vision to provide a wide range of modular kitchen designs. The goal was clear: to do our best and to become the best! We now specialize in innovative planning components and flexible modules suited to your vision for a personalized kitchen space. Over the years, our team has grown to more than 25 experienced professionals to cater to your dream home. Since our inception, we have catered to more than 1500 clients across the country!

WHAT WE PROVIDE

Casa Provides Creative solutions by professional designers

We understand that kitchens are one of the most used spaces in a home. It acts as a multipurpose space and, thus, requires easily functional cabinetry and an efficient storage system. To facilitate this, we provide top to bottom solutions and products for all your needs for a personalized kitchen design in the latest finishes. We also deal in solid Caesarstone, Quartz stone, and Corian worktop finishes, which can be customized with your preferred splashbacks, matching the interior design of your home.

We pay attention to every client’s vision for their spaces, be it their home or office. Our team ensures ample satisfaction with the end product, managing its proper installation and excellent after-sales service. After all, our main point of focus in our relationship with our clients is trust; we trust your choices and hope that you trust our execution process
